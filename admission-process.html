Entrance Requirements
1. Foreign nationality and 18 years old at least at the time of application.
2. Completed an education equivalent to 12-year education system in Japan.
3. Eagerness to learn Japanese and health condition suitable for study.

Application Process
Admission consultation → Interview → Interview result → Document inspection → Submission of Application for Certificate of Eligibility (COE) to Immigration Office → COE result announcement → School fee payment → Visa processing → Arrival

Short Term Admission Information
Entrance Requirements
Short term visa, Dependent visa, Working holiday visa holder
Application Period
April, July, October, January
There are basic classes for April, July, October intake. But for January intake, applicants are required to have at least N4 level in Japanese language. However, this is based on the capacity per intake every year. Please contact us in advance.
Application Process
Applicants living abroad
Document submission → Document inspection → School fee payment → Admission Certificate issuance → Arrival
Applicants living in Japan
Admission consultation → Level Check → Class observation → School fee payment → Entrance
