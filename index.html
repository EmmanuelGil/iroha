Student Recruitment

International Exchange

We have been accepting international students from over 30 countries and regions, including those throughout Asia.
Small-group class

Classes are kept small with a maximum of 20 students per class. The homeroom teacher confirms each student's understanding as the lesson progresses.
Visa Issuance Rate

If you choose "Iroha Japanese Language School," which is certified as a "Proper School" (適正校) by the Immigration Bureau, the procedures for obtaining the necessary residence status for studying abroad will be smoother and require fewer documents.
External exam preparation EJU/JLPT

Our curriculum focuses on improving your scores by strengthening your knowledge of Japanese and general subjects through repeated practice using past exam questions.
Experienced teaching staff

The instructors, who have built a solid career as Japanese language teachers, conduct enjoyable and engaging lessons. Not only do they study how to teach Japanese, but they also continuously learn to understand the diverse backgrounds of their students.
Extracurricular activities

BBQ, Cultural Experiences, Excursion, Field Trip
Admission Procedures

Guidance of Higher Education

We provide students who wish to pursue higher education with abundant information and experience to help them find the best path forward. We offer personalized guidance for every step of the process, including consultations on academic advancement, preparation of application documents, essay guidance, and interview preparation.

Easy Access

Iroha Japanese Language School is located less than a 5-minute walk from JR Hakata Station, the gateway to Kyushu. This prime location offers the perfect environment for international students to find part-time jobs and engage in cultural exchange.

Comprehensive Support for Learning and Daily Life

Our dedicated team provides support for your visa and admission procedures, and helps you with any issues in your daily life. We offer assistance in seven languages, including English, Chinese, and Vietnamese.
