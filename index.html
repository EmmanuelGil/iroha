    Let us study the Japanese language in a good quality education environment in Fukuoka!

    Students from all over the world

    ENROLLMENT

    International Exchange Environment

    Students from more than 30 countries, including from Asia, are coming to study.
    Small Classroom Sizes

    Classes are set up small sized with about 20 students per class. Therefore, the homeroom teacher can follow up the progress of study of each student
    Visa Issuance Rate

    Iroha Japanese Language School, which has been certified as an "appropriate school" by the Immigration Bureau, allows for smooth procedures for obtaining the residence status necessary for studying abroad with a small number of documents.
    Examination of EJU/ JLPT

    By repeating practice based on past questions, our curriculum aims to improve your score by strengthening Japanese and general subjects.
    Experienced Teachers

    You can enjoy lessons provided by our experienced teachers. Besides lessons, our teachers are willing to listen to your opinions.
    Outdoor Activities
    Admission process

    Guidance of Higher Education

    Based on personal interviews, the teachers will give our students guidance on the thesis and interview for a higher education that is suitable for each student. There are many partner-schools where admit our students with a Letter of Recommendation from the school.

    Easy Access

    Iroha is located near JR Hakata Station, the gateway of Kyushu Island about 5 minutes by walk. It is the best location for international students to work part-time and experience Japanese culture.

    Useful Support of Study and life

    We offer appropriate support when you have any problems or to your daily life, to your visa and admission procedures. Our staff can speak 7 languages, including English, Chinese, and Vietnamese.
